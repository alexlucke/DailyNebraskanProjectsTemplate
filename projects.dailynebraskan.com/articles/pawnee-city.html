'We're Survivors'

One family's fight to keep a town alive with local shop

By James Wooldridge
Photos by James Wooldridge

Marie Glenn and her husband retired in their hometown of Pawnee City, Neb. to help revive the struggling community. When the local hardware store was going out of business, they bought it to keep customers in the area. Now, a new Dollar General in the town threatens local businesses.
